Methodology & Credits

─── Methodology ───────────────────────────────────────────

Objective:  max (μ − rf)ᵗw − γ · wᵗΣw
            subject to: Σwᵢ = 1,  0 ≤ wᵢ ≤ max_wt

μ (expected returns): EWMA of historical log-returns (hl = 252 trading days),
  then James-Stein cross-sectional shrinkage toward equal-weighted mean:
  μ_eff = (1 − α)·μ_EWMA + α·μ̅   where α = shrinkage parameter.

Σ (covariance): EWMA of outer products of demeaned returns (hl = 21 trading days),
  annualised × 252. Regularised with ε·I to ensure positive definiteness.

Optimiser: projected gradient ascent with simplex+box projection
  (Condat 2016, binary search on dual variable).

Walk-forward: rebalance on first trading day of each month/quarter.
  Performance and weights evaluated out-of-sample.

Data is fetched via a Cloudflare Worker proxy (API keys are server-side only,
  never exposed to the browser). Results are cached across all users via
  Cloudflare KV, and locally in your browser's localStorage.
Computation runs entirely in your browser.
