Projects

Statistics · Sports Analytics

Cricket Prediction Model

A ball-by-ball Markov chain model for T20 cricket that estimates expected runs distributions per batting style and boundary pattern, benchmarked against Polymarket prediction market odds.

Computational Chemistry · RL

Molecular Generation for Photoswitches

Used REINVENT 4, AstraZeneca's deep generative chemistry framework, to discover novel photoswitch candidates, steering molecular exploration with reinforcement learning guided by DFT-computed optical switching properties.

Finance · Optimization

Markowitz Engine

An interactive walk-forward portfolio optimizer running entirely in the browser. Choose from 242 equities and ETFs across 17 sectors including bonds, gold, and commodities. Tune risk aversion, James-Stein shrinkage, and rebalance frequency, then run a full backtest with performance charts, weights heatmap, and forecast accuracy diagnostics.
